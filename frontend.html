Campaigns

Co-curricular activities

We provide yoga, drama, dance, music and sports. This socialization is an essential part of life and helps in the learning process. Co-curricular activities bring social and intellectual skills, moral values and character appeal in students.

Independent Living Skills

Training is provided to develop and enhance the ability to take care of themselves, their own needs of hygiene and nutrition, travelling and security.

Placements

Skills are taught to all for becoming active members in society to earn a livelihood. They are sent on job training to hotels and restaurants for housekeeping and kitchen skills, to offices for data entry and to train as peons and telephone operators.

About Us

why choose us?

MISSION

Help persons with disability to learn, endure and feel empowered to venture out and achieve their dreams, hence creating and equal place in society.

VISION

To create a life without limits for persons with disability by improving their quality of life with functional, physical and vocational training.

PHYSIOTHERAPY

Helps focus on function, movement and optimal use of persons potential. Helps maintain and restore physical, psychological and social wellbeing within all environments.
